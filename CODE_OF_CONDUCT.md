# Community Code of Conduct

Be respectful, constructive, and welcoming to contributors from water operations, engineering, education, visualization, and software backgrounds. Critique ideas and evidence rather than people. Harassment, discrimination, threats, doxxing, and publication of sensitive facility information are not acceptable.

Repository maintainers may edit or remove contributions and restrict participation when needed to protect people, the project, or drinking-water facilities. Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner when a private contact route is published.
